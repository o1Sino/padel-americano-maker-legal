Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Padel Americano Maker ("the App") is a free, ad-supported mobile application for organizing padel tournaments. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

Data We Collect

Tournament Data: Tournament names, player names, scores, and match results are stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to any external server unless you explicitly choose to share results.

Advertising: We use Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data to serve relevant ads. We request non-personalized ads by default. You can learn more about Google's data practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data We Do NOT Collect

• We do not require user accounts or registration
• We do not collect email addresses or personal information
• We do not track your location
• We do not sell any data to third parties
• We do not use analytics or tracking beyond what AdMob provides

Data Storage

All tournament data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. If you uninstall the App, all data is permanently deleted. We do not maintain any cloud backup of your data. You can also delete all data from within the app via Profile → Delete All Data.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob: Used to display banner advertisements. AdMob's privacy policy applies to data collected through ads. We block the following ad categories to maintain a sports-appropriate experience: gambling, dating, and political advertising.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your data. You can delete all data by using the "Delete All Data" option in the app's Profile section, or by uninstalling the App.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated policy will be available within the App and at this URL.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the App Store or Google Play listing.
