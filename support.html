Padel Americano Maker — Support

How to Use

Create a tournament, add players, and start playing. Tap the scores on the court cards to enter results. The app auto-calculates opponent scores and tracks standings in real-time.

Frequently Asked Questions

My data disappeared

Tournament data is stored locally on your device. If you uninstalled and reinstalled the app, your data was deleted. Unfortunately, there is no cloud backup in this version.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Profile → Delete All Data. This permanently removes all tournaments, scores, and saved player names.

The app crashed

Please try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, uninstall and reinstall. We apologize for any inconvenience.
